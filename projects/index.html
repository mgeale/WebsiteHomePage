Web Apps

Journal of Captain James Cook

On his first voyage to the South Pacific, Capt. James Cook kept a journal for documenting what happened. This web app presents his journal entries in a format that is easy to follow using Google Maps.


Journal files provided by Australian National Library. Built using Polymer.

Exhibition of War Photographs

In Sydney, 1919 an exhibition opened showcasing the work of Frank Hurley, a war photographer for the Australian Imperial Force during World War I. This web app presents the photographs in an easy to view format.


Photographs supplied by The State Library of New South Wales. Built using AngularJS and Bootstrap.

Collection of Natural History Drawings

Collection of drawings from the 19th century of Australian flora and fauna.


Using AngularJS, Materialize. Materials provided by New South Wales State Library.
