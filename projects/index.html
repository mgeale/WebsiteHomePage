Journal of Captain James Cook

On his first voyage to the South Pacific, Capt. James Cook kept a journal documenting the ship's course, interactions with indigenous peoples and unexpected events. This web app presents his journal entries in a format that is easy to follow using Google Maps.


Journal files provided by Australian National Library. Built using Polymer.

Exhibition of War Photographs

Captain Frank Hurley was a World War I photographer on deployment from August 1917 and August 1918. In 1919 an exhibition opened in Sydney to showcase his work. This web app republishes those photographs using modern web technologies.


Photographs supplied by The State Library of New South Wales. Built using AngularJS and Bootstrap.

Collection of Natural History Drawings

Collection of drawings from the 19th century of Australian flora and fauna.


Using AngularJS, Materialize. Materials provided by New South Wales State Library.
