Journal of Captain Cook

On his voyage to the South Pacific, Capt. James Cook kept a journal for documenting the ship's course and discoveries. This web app presents his journal entries in a format that is easy to follow using Google Maps.


Journal files provided by Australian National Library. Built with Polymer.

Exhibition of War Photographs

In Sydney, 1919 an exhibition took place showcasing the work of Frank Hurley, a war photographer for the Australian Imperial Force during World War I. This web app presents his photographs in an easy to view format.


Photographs supplied by The State Library of New South Wales. Built with Angular.
